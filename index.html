Who We Are

Tradition &
Innovation
in Every Bite

SIG-MA Incorporated — short for Sisig Pang Masa — was founded to bring beloved Filipino cuisines to everyone through innovative preparation. The Sigma (Σ) in our name represents the sum of our mission: combining cultural nostalgia with modern culinary craft.


Established in December 2025, we operate out of the Public Market of Talisay, Batangas, serving locals, students, tourists, and food lovers who crave something authentically Filipino — yet refreshingly new.
